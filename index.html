What is Osaka Punch?

Osaka Punch is an Australian band created in 2012, utilizing an interesting combination of funky bass, and heavy grooves, intermingled with a mix of strong versatile jazzy vocals. Their interesting combination of genres and musical elements is often filled with refreshingly humorous and satirical lyrics and subject matter. Their well made songs and incredible musicianship has allowed them to tour Australia, the UK, and Scandinavia.

Why I Made This Site?

I made this site about Osaka Punch because I enjoy their music a lot and thing they are underrated. I think their music deserves to be more popular than it is, and I would love to see them live someday.
